# Sprint Proposal Template

> This is the narrative template. For the machine-readable issue form, use [sprint-proposal.yml](../.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/sprint-proposal.yml).

---

## Title

A 5–10 word name for the sprint.

## Vertical

Pick one: Verifiable Credentials & Identity · Private Credit & Underwriting · Regulated Privacy & Selective Disclosure · Institutional Settlement & PoR · RWA Attestations · Private Voting & DAO Governance · ZK Audits & Privacy-Preserving Analytics

## Problem

In 2–3 sentences: what gap does this close? Who feels the pain today?

## Deliverable

One concrete artifact you will ship in 2–4 weeks. Be specific: a working CLI, a protocol spec + reference implementation, a demo web app, a circuit + test vectors, etc.

## Scope boundaries

What is **out** of scope for this sprint? List at least three things you will not do.

## Team

- Lead: name, GitHub handle, short bio
- Other contributors (if any): same
- Availability: hours/week commitment per person

## Timeline

- Sprint length: 2, 3, or 4 weeks
- Planned start: YYYY-MM-DD
- Weekly checkpoints: what you expect to have by end of each week

## Primitives and dependencies

Which Zcash primitives, libraries, or external services does this use?

## Risks

Top three risks and how you will mitigate each.

## Next-step plan

What will **you** do with this artifact after the sprint? The lab does not route projects anywhere; this is about your intended next step.

- [ ] Keep maintaining it as an independent open-source project
- [ ] Apply for grants or funding elsewhere (on my own initiative)
- [ ] Hand it off to an existing project or team
- [ ] Archive as a reference implementation
- [ ] Undecided — will figure out during the sprint
